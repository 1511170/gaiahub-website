3\. Gaia Investments Fund

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/OIG4.webp)

Purpose driven aligned profit generating organisations and objects that do not fully align with the gaia commons profit sharing agreement.

Operating under the Global Commons Framework, this component serves as an investment framework for Gaia Funds. It supports regenerative and income-generating initiatives and projects globally, aligned with the Gaia Commons principles.
