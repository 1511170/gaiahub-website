**Gaia Commons:** a membership eco-system of conscious individuals who are aligned and committed in their pursuit of a well nourished planet.  Members commit to contribute social and financial capital to the Shared Basket of Abundance for the good of the whole and the future generations. Members receive an abundance of benefits such as meaningful experiences, funding, work and exchange opportunities, community belonging and deep connection, discounted prices on regenerative goods and services, and social benefits including holistic education and alternative health services and retirement care.

Gaia Commons can also be understood as a network of members (beneficiaries and benefactors with various types of memberships) that agree to radically collaborate to realize effective coordination as we invest in regenerative projects, goods and services, including land that we are commonly stewarding for perpetuity.  Project areas include:  Housing and Infrastructure, Agriculture, Education, Technology, Holistic Health, Ecotourism, Businesses & Experiences in general.  All these goods and assets are stewarded by the Commons using a holonic, distributed and dynamic governance, economic and communication system, for more efficient connection, collaboration and coordination (CO3).

DEFINITION

GaiaHub is an Innovative Network EcoSystem With The Mission To Bridge Worlds In Service To All Forms Of Life

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/OIG3.47Qk6wRqWpthyblHed.webp)

Gaia is a for purpose ecosystem devoted to fostering holistic integral welfare.

Embodying the Gaia Commons Governance we are a global holonic distributed network ecosystem of projects, organizations, regenerators, entrepreneurs and aligned companies.

We envision a future where the planet flourishes, where vibrant ecosystems and thriving communities coexist in abundance.
