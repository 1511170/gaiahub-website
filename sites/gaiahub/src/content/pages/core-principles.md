Core Principles of Gaia Commons Hub



Holonic Governance:

We support the interconnectedness of social,

environmental, cultural and economic systems.

[READ MORE](https://gaiahub.net.co/holonic-governance-guide/)



Regeneration:

We go beyond sustainability. We believe in regenerative practices that restore and enhance the natural environment, replenish resources, and promote ecological resilience.

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/serv_p1.png)

Empowerment:

We empower community-led initiatives that foster self-reliance, equitable participation, and cultural preservation and resilience.



Innovation and Adaptability

We embrace innovation and emerging technologies.

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/eduRecurso-38@4x.png)

Catalyze Human Potential:

Through our bio-regional network and  DHEs, we connect individuals with specialised skills, knowledge, and expertise, to contribute to the movement.

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/03/manosRecurso-22@4x.png)

Bio-Regional Collaboration:

By connecting bio-regions across the globe, we foster knowledge sharing, cross-cultural understanding, and collective action towards regenerative practices.



Embrace New Tech

We embrace blockchain, decentralised finance (DeFi), tokens and NFTs for transparent and efficient financial systems that empower communities.

![](https://gaiahub.net.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/07/gaiacommons_logo.png)
